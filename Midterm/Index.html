General Laser Cutting Guidelines
Quick Links

Written by Nataly Li

  • Material should be flat and on the approved list
  • Cut lines should be no closer than half the thickness
    of the material i.e. if your material is 0.25 inches
    thick, then your cut lines should be no closer than
    0.125 inches
  • Do not cut on the edge of the material, leave a
    0.125 inch border
  • Always turn on the filters before a print. Acrylic
    fumes are not good to breathe
